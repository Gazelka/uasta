Frequently Asked Questions

What is UASTA?

UASTA stands for the Ukrainian Association of Students in Austria, a student-led initiative supporting Ukrainian students across Austria.

Who can join?

Any Ukrainian student currently studying or planning to study in Austria is welcome to join us!

How can I participate in events?

Follow us on Telegram or Instagram to stay updated. You can also join our group for announcements and sign-up links.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is free. We welcome volunteers and active participants to help grow our community.
